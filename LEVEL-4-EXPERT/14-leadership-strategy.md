# Leadership & Strategy for ML Teams

## Overview
Guidance for technical leaders running ML teams: roadmaps, ethics, hiring, cross-functional collaboration, and technical debt management.

## Learning Objectives
- Build an ML roadmap aligned to business goals
- Understand ethical considerations and responsible AI practices
- Create hiring and onboarding plans for ML teams
- Manage technical debt and maintainability of ML systems
- Run post-mortems and continuous improvement cycles

## Topics / Exercises
1. Create a 6-month roadmap for an ML product
2. Draft an ethical checklist for data and models
3. Design a hiring rubric for ML engineers and data scientists
4. Simulate stakeholder alignment sessions
5. Develop compliance and legal review process for AI vendors

---

> Useful for senior engineers and managers transitioning to leadership roles.
