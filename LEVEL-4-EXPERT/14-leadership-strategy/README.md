# AI/ML Leadership - Module 08\n\n## Overview\n\nThis module prepares you for leadership roles in AI/ML organizations. Learn how to build high-performing teams, drive strategy, manage stakeholders, and ensure ethical AI practices.\n\n**Target Audience**: Senior engineers, tech leads, team leads, managers\n**Duration**: 8-10 hours\n**Difficulty**: Advanced\n\n---\n\n## Module Contents\n\n### 📓 Notebooks\n\n#### 1. **ML Team Management** (`01-ml-team-management.ipynb`)\nBuilding and managing high-performing ML teams.\n\n**Topics Covered**:\n- Team composition and hiring\n- Delegation and task management\n- Performance management\n- Career development\n- Handling technical challenges\n\n**Key Concepts**:\n- Different roles in ML teams\n- 1-on-1 meetings and feedback\n- Building psychological safety\n- Balancing feature delivery and tech debt\n\n**Practical Guides**:\n- Hiring rubric template\n- 1-on-1 agenda template\n- Performance review framework\n- Career path planning\n\n**Estimated Time**: 3-4 hours\n\n---\n\n#### 2. **ML Strategy and Roadmapping** (`02-ml-strategy.ipynb`)\nAligning ML with business goals and execution.\n\n**Topics Covered**:\n- Business alignment\n- Strategic roadmapping\n- ROI and metrics\n- Stakeholder management\n- Risk management\n- Budgeting and resource planning\n\n**Key Concepts**:\n- ML use case identification\n- OKRs and KPIs\n- ROI calculation\n- Stakeholder mapping\n- Canary deployments and A/B testing\n\n**Practical Guides**:\n- Roadmap template\n- OKR framework\n- Stakeholder communication plan\n- Risk matrix\n\n**Estimated Time**: 3-4 hours\n\n---\n\n#### 3. **Ethics and Responsible AI** (`03-ethics-responsible-ai.ipynb`)\nEnsuring ethical, fair, and responsible AI systems.\n\n**Topics Covered**:\n- AI ethics framework\n- Bias detection and mitigation\n- Fairness and explainability\n- Privacy and security\n- Governance and compliance\n\n**Key Concepts**:\n- Demographic parity and equalized odds\n- LIME and SHAP explanations\n- Differential privacy\n- Model cards and documentation\n- Audit processes\n\n**Practical Tools**:\n- Fairness libraries (fairlearn, aif360)\n- Explainability tools (LIME, SHAP)\n- Risk assessment templates\n- Compliance checklists\n\n**Estimated Time**: 2-3 hours\n\n---\n\n## Leadership Skills Breakdown\n\n### People Leadership (40%)\n- Hiring and building teams\n- 1-on-1 meetings\n- Feedback and coaching\n- Career development\n- Conflict resolution\n\n### Strategic Thinking (30%)\n- Business alignment\n- Roadmapping\n- Resource allocation\n- Risk management\n- Decision making\n\n### Technical Credibility (20%)\n- Understanding ML work\n- Architecture reviews\n- Technical discussions\n- Unblocking teams\n- Staying current\n\n### Ethics and Culture (10%)\n- Responsible AI\n- Inclusive teams\n- Psychological safety\n- Continuous improvement\n\n---\n\n## Learning Path\n\n### For New Team Leads\n1. Start with **Team Management** (people skills)\n2. Study **Strategy** (business alignment)\n3. Learn **Ethics** (responsible practices)\n\n### For Experienced Leaders\n1. Review **Team Management** (best practices)\n2. Deep dive **Strategy** (advanced planning)\n3. Master **Ethics** (governance)\n\n### For Individual Contributors Moving to Leadership\n1. **Team Management** - new skills needed\n2. **Strategy** - business perspective\n3. **Ethics** - leadership responsibility\n\n---\n\n## Key Outcomes\n\nAfter completing this module, you'll be able to:\n\n✅ **People Management**:\n- Build diverse, high-performing teams\n- Conduct effective 1-on-1s\n- Provide meaningful feedback\n- Develop team members' careers\n- Handle underperformance\n\n✅ **Strategy**:\n- Align ML initiatives with business goals\n- Create compelling roadmaps\n- Calculate ROI and business impact\n- Manage stakeholders effectively\n- Mitigate risks\n\n✅ **Ethics**:\n- Identify and mitigate bias\n- Ensure model fairness\n- Explain model decisions\n- Protect privacy\n- Establish governance\n\n---\n\n## Core Competencies\n\n### Technical Understanding (70%)\n- Can discuss ML tradeoffs\n- Understand team's work\n- Make informed decisions\n- Code reviews (when needed)\n- Architecture discussions\n\n### Communication (80%)\n- Clear vision and goals\n- Regular feedback\n- Transparent decisions\n- Engaging presentations\n- Written communication\n\n### Emotional Intelligence (85%)\n- Empathy for team members\n- Self-awareness\n- Relationship building\n- Conflict navigation\n- Motivating others\n\n### Strategic Thinking (75%)\n- Long-term vision\n- Business acumen\n- Resource optimization\n- Risk management\n- Decision making\n\n---\n\n## Practical Exercises\n\n### 1.
